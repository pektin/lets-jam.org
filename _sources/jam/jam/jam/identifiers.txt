.. _jam-identifiers:

Identifiers
###########

Identifiers are used to identify values in Jam.

Syntax
======

Identifiers start with any ascii letter (``a-zA-Z``) or an underscore (``_``).
After the first character, digits (``0-9``) may also be part of the name.

Examples
--------

::

    fooBar
    _foo_bar_fizz_bazz_
    _9
