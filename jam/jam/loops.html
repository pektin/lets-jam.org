10.1. Loops¶

Loops group a section of instructions and loops them. Jam has three kinds of loops. A generic loops without a condition, a while loop with a simple condition and a for loop for interaction.

While inside of a loop there are multiple other control structures that become value. These are the break and continue statements.

10.1.1. Syntax¶

Loop        ::=  GenericLoop | WhileLoop | ForLoop
GenericLoop ::=  "loop"
                   InstructionSet
                 "end"
WhileLoop   ::=  "while" Value
                   InstructionSet
                 "end"
ForLoop     ::=  "for" Variable "in" Value
                   InstructionSet
                 "end"
